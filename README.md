# **Summer Project 2025: Medical Applications of Nuclear Physics**

This project is part of the Radionuclide Production and Speciation research theme at the Norwegian Nuclear Research Centre (NNRC). The focus is on producing and studying radionuclides for medical applications, including diagnostics and therapy, with special attention to Auger electron emitters and theranostics pairs.

Summer students will analyze experimental data to determine radionuclide production cross sections and assist in experiments using the HUNT neutron generator, a national facility hosted by UiO.

Supervision by Prof. Sunniva Siem and PhD students Elise Malmer Martinsen and Hannah Ekeberg.
